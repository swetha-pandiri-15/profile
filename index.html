About

Bio

I'm a finance systems and analytics leader who loves turning messy reporting into reliable decision intelligence. I began my career at Deloitte (USI) working with financial data, where I learned how processes actually break in the real world—inside spreadsheets, handoffs, and unclear ownership. To anchor my technical toolkit, I completed a master’s in Business Analytics at the University of Illinois, focusing on practical modeling, data engineering fundamentals, and communicating insights for operators and executives. Today, as FP&A Business Systems Manager at Kaiser Aluminum, I lead enterprise reporting and automation initiatives—designing architectures, Power BI experiences, and governance practices that help CFOs and controllers move from reactive reporting to repeatable, CFO‑ready insight. My work centers on scalability, clarity, and change adoption so solutions stick long after the first demo.
