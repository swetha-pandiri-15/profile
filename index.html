About

Swetha Pandiri is a Financial Systems Manager at Kaiser Aluminum, where she leads enterprise reporting and automation initiatives. She bridges finance strategy with technology execution, designing scalable solutions that transform manual processes into insight-driven systems, enabling finance to shift from reactive reporting to strategic decision-making.
